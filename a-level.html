A Level

LACAS A Level has consistently led the way in innovation and academic excellence. We were the first school in Lahore to offer A Level to girls, pioneering opportunities for female students in higher education.

LACAS was also the first to introduce parliamentary-style debates in Pakistan, fostering critical thinking and public speaking skills. Additionally, we established the country’s first-ever Liberal Arts A Level program and were proud to be the first school to introduce TEDx, providing a platform for students to engage in global ideas and discussions.

LACAS Guidance Counselling Office

The LACAS A Level Guidance Counselor Office offers comprehensive support for academic and career planning. We assist with subject selection, college/university choices, and application processes (Common Application, UCAS, OUAC).

Our team helps students draft strong college essays and personal statements, build profiles through internships and volunteer work, and prepare for standardized tests like the SAT and IELTS.

On-campus career advisory sessions provide additional guidance to ensure students are well-prepared for their future paths.

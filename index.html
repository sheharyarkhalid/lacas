Technology

Innovation and Inspiration

At LACAS, we are dedicated to integrating technology into every classroom, ensuring our students are prepared to thrive in a digital world. Our cutting-edge Eyeris Labs foster hands-on learning and experimentation, while the Robotics Through Art program combines creativity and engineering to develop critical skills through engaging STEAM projects. With the Oxford Reading Buddy Programme, students enjoy personalized reading experiences that enhance vocabulary and comprehension. By embracing advanced technologies across all subjects, we lead with innovation, empowering students for a tech-driven future with confidence and creativity.

academic excellence

Studies Meets Ambition

At LACAS, we take pride in our outstanding results in Cambridge International Examinations (CIE), which reflect our commitment to academic excellence. Our students consistently achieve top scores, allowing them to gain admission to some of the best colleges and universities around the world.

This success not only opens doors to higher education but also instills confidence in our students, empowering them to pursue their ambitions fearlessly. We believe that with the right support and guidance, every student can realize their potential and achieve their dreams.

Sports

Champions in Training

At LACAS, we believe that sports are essential for holistic development, fostering teamwork, discipline, and resilience in our students. Our state-of-the-art facilities, including a futsal court, basketball courts, and football fields, provide ample opportunities for students to engage in physical activity and develop their athletic skills.

Events like the LACAS Super League and Power Up invite students from various schools to participate, creating a vibrant culture of competition and sportsmanship. These experiences not only enhance physical fitness but also promote lifelong values such as leadership and camaraderie, preparing our students to excel both on and off the field.

Extra curricular

Explore, Express, Excel

At LACAS, we recognize the importance of extracurricular activities in nurturing well-rounded individuals. Our vibrant programs in debate, theater, and music encourage students to explore their passions and express themselves creatively.

Events such as our annual plays and the LACAS Model United Nations (MUN) provide platforms for students to showcase their talents and engage with real-world issues, promoting critical thinking and collaboration. Through these enriching experiences, we empower our students to excel not only academically but also as expressive, confident individuals ready to take on the challenges of the future.
